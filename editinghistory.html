Editing History

The enriched history of work displayed by you and your team

Viewing The Editing History

When on GitHub the left side of the page has a tab that says changes and history. If you select the history tab the entire editing history is displayed providing all changes and commits from the user who pushed it. The history provides timestamps and the changes of what happened showing the code on the right side of the page.

As shown above the editing history also highlights what code was changed and what was deleted with corresponding colors of green and red

What is The Purpose?

The editing history provides insight by your teamates and even upper managment on the process of developing your code. Editng history can be a great tool to provide credit to each person on a team and can help find errors when they occur.
