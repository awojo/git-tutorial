Things To Consider Before Getting A Dog

Many people think that it would be so great to get a dog. However, taking care of another living creature is a significant responsibility.

Unless you plan to allow your dog to relieve itself inside your house, you will need to plan to let the dog out at specific moments throughout the day and night.

How Often You Will Need To Let A Dog Outside To Go Potty

Here are some rough estimates of how often a dog will need to be let outside for pottying:

Morning, upon waking 6:00 AM
After first meal 7:00 AM
Midday 1 10:00 AM
Afternoon, when you arrive home from work or school 4:00 PM
